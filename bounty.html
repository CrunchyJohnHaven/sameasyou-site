Rules

  • Submission. Open a GitHub issue at calm-vault/issues with a reproducible demonstration. Email bounty@sameasyou.ai if the issue itself would constitute responsible-disclosure harm; we will coordinate private review.
  • Public attestation. Every successful break is publicly attested on the Origin-Bound Attestation Chain (OBAC) and acknowledged on this page. Researcher attribution honors stated preferences.
  • Statute of limitations. 90 days from public site launch (2026-05-11) for the initial pool. Submissions after that window remain accepted; payouts depend on the renewed pool.
  • Originality. First valid submission in submission order wins the tier. Duplicates receive public credit without payout.
  • Scope. The published reference implementation in calm-vault and the protocol specification it embodies. Out-of-scope: third-party dependencies, infrastructure (Cloudflare, GitHub), and social-engineering attacks against the authors.
  • Safe harbor. Good-faith research conducted within these rules is authorized. We will not pursue legal action against researchers who follow responsible disclosure.

Why a bounty

A claim that cannot be falsified is not a claim. The bounty is the falsifiability test made financial.

We make a tight, narrow claim: a deployment-relevant subset of AI alignment — the coordination and accountability subset. Each falsification target above corresponds to a specific failure mode (F1, F2, F5, F6) drawn from the protocol's foundational claims. Break any of them and you have moved the field forward; we pay you for moving the field forward.
