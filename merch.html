Where the money goes

Every dollar above unit cost flows to the commons.

Unit cost (Printful / similar): ~$10-12 per tee for blank + print + shipping. We sell at $25. The ~$13-15 margin is split:

  • $5-7 to the bounty pool — top up the $100 falsification prize, scale to higher tiers ($1K, $10K, $100K) as merch sales grow
  • $3-5 to the competition prize pool — fund the next $1,000 design competition + runners-up
  • $1-3 to apprentice stipends — every shirt sold pays for an hour of paid test-task time at apprentice tier
  • $1 to the designer who made the shirt — recurring royalty for the original art
  • $0 to the founder — yes, even the founder. The chain is the proof.

That is the technosocialist revenue model in plain English. You buy a t-shirt; the money funds the movement; nobody at the top skims. You can verify the ledger any time at sameasyou.ai/live.

Hiring the designer

The mockups above are SVG placeholders done in a hurry. Final art needs a real designer. We are paying for the work.

The brief: take these 8 t-shirt concepts and make them iconic. Improve typography, illustration, color, layout. Aesthetic ranges from Russian-constructivist to 1970s-Berkeley-protest to Python-clean-design. Use what fits each shirt's tone. Deliver print-ready files (PNG 4500×5400 + SVG source) plus mockups on a black t-shirt.

The deal: $400 flat for the set, paid Wise / PayPal / USDC on delivery. PLUS a recurring $1 royalty per shirt sold (your work, your share). PLUS OBAC attestation as a senior contributor. PLUS first dibs on the next design batch (we will need totes, stickers, hoodies).

How to apply: send 2-3 portfolio links + your rate justification + your favorite shirt of the 9 above (you'll start with that one) to designer@sameasyou.ai. We pick within 7 days.

Where we hire from: anywhere. We expect strong applications from designers in India, the Philippines, Pakistan, Brazil, Eastern Europe, and across Africa — places where world-class design talent is undervalued by the existing market. We pay USD-flat regardless of geography. Time zone is irrelevant if work is good. The $400 + $1-per-shirt royalty is the same offer everywhere.

How the store will work

Platform: Printful + Shopify, OR Spring (formerly Teespring) for zero-inventory print-on-demand. We pay nothing until a shirt sells. The platform handles printing, shipping, returns. We handle the art + the brand.

Launch: estimated June 2026, after the designer ships the final art and we have a stripe of OBAC-attested ledger entries showing how merch revenue flows to the commons.
