How to use

License: CC-BY-4.0. Print them. Wheatpaste them. Put them on t-shirts. Make stickers. Print them as A4 / Tabloid / 11×17 / A2 — the SVGs scale to any size without quality loss. Modify them if you can do better. Send us the improvements and we'll add them to the set.

Attribution: "Same As You · sameasyou.ai" anywhere on the artifact is enough. We are not litigious. Use them.

Right-click any poster above → "Save Image As" to download the SVG. Or open the SVG in a browser and print to PDF / poster size.

If you're an ad agency

We've reached out to several creative shops for a minimum-value quote on a 30-60 day campaign. If we missed you and you want in, reply to press@sameasyou.ai with your engagement floor and June-July capacity.

The work: take these six starter posters and make six hundred. Make them better than ours. Translate them. Adapt them for X, Instagram, LinkedIn, Reddit, billboards, transit, T-shirts. Take "We're all AI interns" from inside-baseball to dinner-table.

Contribute a poster

The poster set is open-source. Pull requests welcome at github.com/CrunchyJohnHaven/sameasyou-site. Add yours to /posters.html. Senior contributors who submit accepted posters get attestation on the chain + revenue share if any commercial reuse happens.
